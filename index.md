# Delete NTSA Questions and Answers Account

Users can request deletion of their **NTSA Questions and Answers** app account and associated data.

## How to request account deletion
Please send an email to:

**info@stephenndegwa.com**

### Email subject:
Delete NTSA App Account

### Include in your email:
- The email address used in the app
- Confirmation that you want your account deleted

## Data that will be deleted
- Email address / username
- Account credentials
- Any saved progress or preferences

## Data retention
- All requested data is permanently deleted within **7 days**
- No personal data is retained after deletion

If you do not have an account, no action is required as no personal data is stored.
